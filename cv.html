Research Experience

  • Dec 2025 - Present: Robust data-use auditing under watermark evasion settings
    • Investigate how adversaries can weaken watermark signals and evade current auditing pipelines, and develop more robust detection strategies for source attribution and compliance verification.
  • Mar 2025 - Present: Black-box auditing of sensitive and copyrighted materials in LLMs
    • Develop entropy-guided rewriting and perturbation-based auditing methods to identify sensitive or copyrighted content in commercial black-box models.
  • Jun 2025 - Nov 2025: Privacy leakage analysis for large reasoning models
    • Study how exposed reasoning traces create new membership leakage channels, and characterize the gap between memorization signals and ordinary reasoning behavior.
    • Representative work was accepted by WWW 2026.
  • Oct 2024 - Feb 2025: Automated pre-training data auditing for black-box LLMs
    • Develop zero-prompt automated detection methods for identifying whether candidate text appears in pre-training corpora, improving scalability and robustness for data-use auditing.
    • Representative work was accepted by IJCAI 2025.
  • Nov 2025 - Present: Security and memory isolation analysis for shared LLM agents
    • Study memory isolation, access control, and information exposure risks in multi-user agent systems, with a focus on secure memory interaction and cross-session safety.
  • Oct 2024 - Present: Robust black-box membership inference with weak supervision
    • Design collaborative weak-supervision frameworks that combine multi-task signals and pseudo labels to improve robustness and reduce false positives in black-box membership inference.
  • Sep 2025 - Present: Adaptive jailbreak detection and safety alignment for LLMs
    • Develop immune-memory-inspired defense and evaluation methods for evolving jailbreak attacks, focusing on adaptive detection, response simulation, and safety-aligned model behavior.
