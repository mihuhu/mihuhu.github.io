About Me

About

  • Ruihan Hu is a Ph.D. candidate in Cyberspace Security at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). She is pursuing her doctorate through a combined Master's-Ph.D. track expected to conclude in June 2027, and received her Bachelor's degree in Internet of Things Engineering from BUPT in June 2022.
  • Her research focuses on security in large language models, especially data privacy attack and defense, data-use auditing, data watermark robustness, LLM memorization, LLM reasoning, and LLM safety alignment.
